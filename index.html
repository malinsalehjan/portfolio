Hi!

I’m Malin, a creative spirit currently pursuing a Master's in Design, Use, and Interaction at the University of Oslo. At the heart of my work is the intersection of humans and technology, a dynamic blend that ignites my passion for crafting solutions that are intuitive and inclusive.

As much as I enjoy visual aesthetics, I am equally fascinated by the complexities of human psychology. Understanding people's inner thoughts and feelings empowers me to craft designs that resonate deeply and evoke meaningful connections. New technologies drives me-—whether it's experimenting with cutting-edge digital tools, exploring creative computing, or delving into multimedia. I believe each medium provides a unique way to communicate ideas, and I relish the challenge of mastering them all in my creative journey.

Always looking for new experiences and challenges!
